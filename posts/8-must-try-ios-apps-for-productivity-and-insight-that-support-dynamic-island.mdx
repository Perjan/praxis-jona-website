When it comes to apps that support [Dynamic Island](/blog/dynamic-island-what-is-it-and-how-does-screen-time-realtime-uses-it) on iOS, there are several options available that can enhance your experience and productivity. Two notable apps that can be included in the list are MoneyCoach and Screen Time Realtime. Let's take a closer look at these apps and six other top choices:

## MoneyCoach
[MoneyCoach](/) is a comprehensive personal finance app that helps you track your expenses, set budgets, and manage your finances effectively. It provides real-time insights into your spending habits and offers features like bill reminders, goal tracking, and investment tracking.
## Screen Time Realtime
[Screen Time Realtime](https://apps.apple.com/app/apple-store/id6452629146?pt=118449936&ct=articles&mt=8) is an app that helps you monitor and manage your screen time usage. It provides real-time data on how much time you spend on different apps and websites, allowing you to set limits and establish healthier digital habits.
## Trello
Trello is a popular project management app that allows you to organize tasks, collaborate with others, and track progress. Its dynamic interface and real-time updates make it a great choice for managing projects and workflows.
## Evernote
Evernote is a versatile note-taking app that lets you capture ideas, create to-do lists, and save important information. With its real-time syncing across devices, you can access your notes from anywhere and collaborate with others seamlessly.
## Todoist
Todoist is a powerful task management app that helps you stay organized and productive. It offers real-time syncing, reminders, and collaboration features, making it easy to manage your tasks and projects effectively.
## Notion
Notion is an all-in-one workspace app that combines note-taking, project management, and collaboration features. Its dynamic interface allows you to create custom layouts and databases, making it a flexible tool for organizing and sharing information.
## Fantastical
Fantastical is a feature-rich calendar app that offers real-time syncing with popular calendar services. It provides a clean and intuitive interface, natural language input, and smart scheduling features, making it a top choice for managing your schedule.
## Forest
Forest is a unique productivity app that helps you stay focused and avoid distractions. It uses a gamified approach, where you grow a virtual tree by staying away from your phone. The app provides real-time feedback and encourages you to build a productive habit.

These eight dynamic island apps on iOS, including MoneyCoach and Screen Time Realtime, offer a range of features to enhance your productivity, manage your finances, and improve your digital well-being.
