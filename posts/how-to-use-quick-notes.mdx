In this tutorial, we are going to learn how we can use Quick Notes to attach notes to your goals, budgets and transactions.

Quick Notes allow you to write why did you create a goal or reflect on the progress of the budget or add additional info to your spending habits. It is completely up to you to do whatever you want.
