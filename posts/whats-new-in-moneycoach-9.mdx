It’s that time of year folks, where we celebrate the launch of the new iOS 17, iPadOS 17 and watchOS 10 releases with a new major update to MoneyCoach. Here’s what’s new in MoneyCoach 9:

## Interactive Widget
Add transactions to any of your categories from any of your MoneyCoach accounts directly from the Home Screen via the new Interactive Widget. Add this new widget to your Home Screen and go wild. Tap and hold it to modify the favourite category and account for even faster entry.

![](/images/features/interactive-widget.png)

## Apple Pay Integration
You can now automatically import transactions from Apple Pay / Wallet via a Shortcut Automation. Setting this up is extremely easy, find a [complete guide on our blog](/guides/how-to-import-apple-pay-wallet-transactions-to-moneycoach) or tutorial section in the app.

## New Watch Experience
We completely redesigned the Apple Watch MoneyCoach experience for watchOS 10 users. A new way to navigate through the app, faster, modern, beautiful and more glanceable.

![](/images/blog-images/moneycoach-watchos-10.jpg)

## StandBy Widgets
Keep a track of your Net Worth and Daily Limit directly on the new Night Stand Widgets.

## Bug Fixes & Improvements

We’ve squashed a lot of minor bugs making the overall app performance better and way more stable. If you spot something wrong in the app, report it and our exterminators will do the rest.

We are super excited for this major release for MoneyCoach and we hope you all like it. Please consider leaving a positive app review as it helps us a lot.

Thanks,  
The MoneyCoach Team
