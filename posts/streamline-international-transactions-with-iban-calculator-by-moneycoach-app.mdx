## Understanding the IBAN

The International Bank Account Number (IBAN) is a standardized format used worldwide to uniquely identify bank accounts. It consists of a country code, two check digits, and the account number itself. The IBAN is vital for ensuring that funds are directed to the correct recipient during cross-border transactions.
