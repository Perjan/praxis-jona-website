Here’s what's new in this update:

- New “Include Transfer” option in the reports filter to show or hide transfers to Goals and Savings accounts from the reports
- Improved the “Add description” field for smaller devices when accessibility options are used
- Improved the Upcoming Bills widget
- Fixed a bug where the payee in a transfer appeared only in one of the transactions
- Fixed a bug with future transactions in a report 

More improvements are coming soon. If you have feedback or suggestions, please submit a support ticket from within the app or via our website. We respond faster on these channels than on negative app reviews.

Best,  
Krist from MoneyCoach
