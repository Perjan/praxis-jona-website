In everyday life, health often becomes a priority only when something is already wrong. Preventive care helps change that. Regular check-ups can identify risks earlier and support better long-term decisions.

## Early detection saves lives

Many conditions, including cardiovascular disease, diabetes, thyroid disorders and some cancers, develop slowly. Symptoms may appear only when the disease is already advanced. Regular examinations can help detect problems earlier.

## Check-ups for adults

In Germany, insured adults can use preventive check-ups depending on age and eligibility. These check-ups are an opportunity to review health status, risk factors and next steps.

## Cancer screening

Cancer screening recommendations differ by age, sex and risk profile. For women, gynecological screening starts early in adulthood. For men, prostate and genital cancer screening becomes relevant later. Colon cancer screening is also an important part of preventive care.

## What you can do for prevention

Prevention is a partnership between patient and physician. Helpful everyday steps include:

- regular check-ups
- balanced nutrition
- regular physical activity
- enough sleep
- stress management
- avoiding smoking and excess alcohol
- keeping vaccinations up to date
- paying attention to mental wellbeing

Prevention is an investment in health and in the future. Medical check-ups help turn that intention into a concrete plan.
