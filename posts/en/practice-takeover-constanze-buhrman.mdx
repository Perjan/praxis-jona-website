I am pleased to announce that as of January 1, 2026, I, Dr. med. Jonida Gjolli, will take over the medical practice of Constanze C. Buhrman.

This change does not mean goodbye. Ms. Buhrman will continue to work as a physician in our practice. This allows us to combine trusted continuity with new impulses for the future of the practice.

We thank Ms. Buhrman for the trust she has placed in Praxis Jona and look forward to continuing our collaboration in the service of your health.

For patients, this primarily means continuity and expanded medical care. We warmly welcome you.

Kind regards,

Dr. med. Jonida Gjolli
