## Digital payments in our practice

Dear patients,

Praxis Jona now supports digital payment options for private services. This means you can pay more conveniently by EC card, credit card, smartphone, smartwatch or online payment link.

## What this means for you

- **Convenient card payment:** You no longer need cash for private services.
- **Mobile payment:** Contactless payment with a smartphone or smartwatch is supported.
- **Faster process:** Payment can be handled more efficiently at the practice.
- **Online payment:** If you receive an invoice with a payment link or QR code, you can pay from home.

## How it works in the practice

When you use a private service, please let our team know that you would like to pay digitally. We use a secure SumUp system for card and mobile payments.

## Online payment

If your invoice includes a QR code or payment link, scan the code or open the link, choose your preferred payment method and complete the payment securely online.

## Security

Your payment data is handled through certified payment systems. If you have questions about card payment or online payment, our team will be happy to help.
