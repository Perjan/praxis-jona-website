I am pleased to announce that as of July 2024, I, Dr. med. Jonida Gjolli, specialist in internal medicine, have taken over the medical practice of Dr. med. Thomas Roessner.

After many years of dedicated work, Dr. Roessner decided to place his practice in my hands. I am grateful for the trust he has shown.

Dr. Roessner made an important contribution to the health of this community through his knowledge and experience. I thank him for his work and wish him all the best for the future.

I am a specialist in internal medicine and have a special focus on thyroid medicine, just as Dr. Roessner did. I look forward to continuing care for his patients with the same commitment and quality.

Thank you for your trust and support. I look forward to welcoming you to the practice.

Kind regards,

Dr. med. Jonida Gjolli
