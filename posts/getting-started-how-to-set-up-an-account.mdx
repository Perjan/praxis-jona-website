Setting up an account is one of the first thingsÂ you might want to do in MoneyCoach. The process is extremely streamlined and easy to do.

**Here's how you do it:**

1- In the Overview, find the Accounts card

2- Tap the "View All" button

3- Tap "+" button

3- Now you can:

- Enter the name of the account
- Enter the initial amount or current balance of the account
- Select a currency (Premium Feature)
- Select the Account type Savings account (Premium Feature)
- Select an icon
- Select the account use, Personal or Business

4- Tap "Save" to add your new account

5- Congratulations, you just added your new Account

The basic version of the app includes two free accounts to use. **Subscribe to MoneyCoach Premium to unlock unlimited accounts.**
