## Understanding Screen Time Passcode
Screen Time passcode is a feature on iPhones that allows users to set limits on app usage, restrict content, and manage device usage for themselves and their family members. It's a helpful tool to maintain a healthy digital balance. However, if you forget the passcode, it can be frustrating, especially if you need to make changes or reset your device.

## Method 1: Resetting Your iPhone Using Recovery Mode

- Backup Your Data:
Before proceeding, ensure you have a recent backup of your iPhone data using iCloud or iTunes.
- Connect to a Computer:
Use a USB cable to connect your iPhone to a computer with iTunes (or Finder on macOS Catalina and later).
- Enter Recovery Mode:
Depending on your iPhone model, follow the appropriate steps to enter Recovery Mode. This usually involves pressing specific buttons in a sequence.
- Restore Your iPhone:
In iTunes or Finder, you'll see an option to restore your iPhone. Follow the prompts to erase your device and install the latest iOS version.
- Set Up Your iPhone:
After the restore process is complete, set up your iPhone as a new device or restore it from a backup.

## Preventive Measures for the Future
