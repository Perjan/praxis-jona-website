We are back with a big new update for MoneyCoach. 

Here’s what’s new:

#### Monterey Support

We’ve updated the app to make the most out of the new things included with the macOS Monterey release. New designs, new icons, new navigation options, new materials and so much more. MoneyCoach on the Mac has never felt any better. Note that this new update is only available for devices running macOS Monterey 12.0 or later.

#### Locations

Yes! When adding a new transaction, you can search for a location and attach it to your transaction. This is just the beginning of this feature as we have plans to expand it even more in the future with additional insights and reports based on your spending locations.

#### Tags

You can now add new tags, edit existing ones, delete and sort them however you want in the Settings.

#### Apple Watch Update

We updated the watch app to support the new larger models. We also added Recent Categories/Subcategories that are perfect when adding a transaction from the watch.

#### New Custom App Icons

We added a couple of new custom app icons requested by the community and a special one featuring our lovely robot mascot.

#### Fixes & Improvements

- We improved the sorting when creating a new payee
- We improved the buttons placement in New Transaction screen
- We improved the CSV importing and exporting
- We fixed a bug that prevented your from using a newly created payee
- We fixed a bug that displayed the wrong localization for categories in Settings
- We fixed a rare bug with the budgets
- We fixed a UI bug in category insights
- We fixed a UI bug in Personalized Goals

We put a lot of love and care into this massive new update and we hope you like what you see. App reviews are like gold to a small app developer. If you like using the app take a minute and leave one, it helps a ton!

If you have feedback or suggestions, please submit a support ticket from within the app. 

Best,  
MoneyCoach team
